Trust & Safety

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026. Your data security is our highest priority.

01. Data Collection

At MoneyMap, we respect your privacy and are committed to transparency. We only collect the information necessary to provide you with a high-quality financial management experience. This includes:

  • Account Information: Your name and email address when you sign up.
  • Financial Data: Transaction amounts, categories, and dates that you manually enter.
  • Preferences: Theme selection and notification settings stored in your local session.
02. Usage of Information

Your data is used solely for generating your spending reports and financial insights. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. Your financial habits are your business, and we intend to keep it that way.

03. Security Measures

We implement military-grade security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Much of your detailed transaction data is stored locally on your device, meaning it stays with you. All communication between your browser and our servers is encrypted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology.

04. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can view, export, or delete your transaction history at any time. If you choose to delete your account, all associated data is purged permanently from our active databases.
