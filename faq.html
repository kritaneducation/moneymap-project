Common Questions

Everything you need to know about navigating your financial roadmap with MoneyMap.

General
How do I add an expense?
Adding an expense is simple. Once you're logged in, navigate to the"Add Expense" tab in your dashboard, enter the amount, select a category, and specify the date. Your balance will update automatically in the reports section.
Can I edit entries after saving them?
Yes, absolutely. You can manage all transactions from the"All Expenses" or"All Income" list pages. Simply use the action menu on any row to edit or delete specific entries.
Account
How do I reset my password?
On the login page, you'll find a"Forgot your password?" link. Follow the prompts to receive a recovery email and set a new secure password meeting our complexity requirements.
Can I change my profile settings?
Yes, the"Settings" page in your dashboard allows you to update your profile photo, personal details, notification preferences, and toggle the dark mode theme.
Data & Privacy
Is my financial data secure?
Security is our top priority. We employ bank-grade SSL encryption and provide multi-factor authentication options to ensure your personal wealth data remains confidential and under your control.
Can I export my reports?
Absolutely. All list pages feature an"Export" button for CSV data, and the comprehensive"Reports" page is optimized for digital sharing or physical printing.
